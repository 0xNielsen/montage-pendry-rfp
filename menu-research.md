## KEY OBSERVATIONS

### 1. Premium Positioning
Both brands favor premium/super-premium spirits (Grey Goose, Belvedere, Roku, WhistlePig) over well brands. This aligns with their luxury hotel positioning.

### 2. Suntory Presence
Roku Gin and Haku Vodka (both Suntory brands) appear at multiple Pendry and Montage properties, indicating potential portfolio placement opportunities.

### 3. House Infusions & Craft Focus
Both brands emphasize house-made syrups, infusions, and seasonal/local ingredients. Cocktail programs are sophisticated and change seasonally.

### 4. Moët & Chandon Partnership (Pendry)
Pendry has an exclusive champagne partnership with Moët & Chandon, including signature vending machines (#ChamPendry) at multiple properties.

### 5. Regional Spirit Programming
- **Montage Los Cabos** – Heavy mezcal/tequila focus
- **Montage Laguna Beach** – Greek spirits (Ouzo, Metaxa, Kleos Mastic)
- **Pendry Baltimore** – Sagamore Rye local partnership
- **Montage Healdsburg** – Wine-centric with mezcal cocktails

### 6. Price Points
- **Pendry cocktails:** $20-26 range
- **Montage cocktails:** $21-28 range
- **Premium tequila pours (Pendry Park City):** $22-415
